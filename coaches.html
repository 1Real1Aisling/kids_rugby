Meet our coaches

Our coaches are essential to young rugby player development. They create a positive, inclusive environment for growth, building skills and confidence. Our experts understand the game and working with young players, creating a supportive atmosphere, empowering players to learn and grow. With our dedicated coaches, your child will receive the highest quality coaching and support to reach their full potential.

Join us today and let our experienced and passionate coaches guide your child to become their best self.

Seán O'Connor

Sean is a highly respected coach in both Ireland and aboroad, having worked with some of the country's top teams over the years. He is known for his tactical acumen and ability to develop young players.

Siobhán Kelly

Siobhan is a former Irish international player who has transitioned into coaching. She has a wealth of experience at the highest level and is known for her ability to get the best out of her players.

Fiona O'Sullivan

Fiona is a former Irish international player who has become a successful coach in her own right. She is highly regarded for her ability to develop players' skills and her focus on team culture.

Liam Murphy

Liam has a wealth of coaching experience at all levels of the sport, from grassroots to the professional game. He is known for his ability to instill discipline and work ethic in his players.

Paddy O'Rourke

Paddy is a veteran coach who has been involved in Irish rugby for decades. He is respected for his vast knowledge of the game and his ability to motivate players to perform at their best.

Laura Finnegan

Laura is a young and dynamic coach who is making a name for herself in the rugby world. She has a reputation for being innovative and creative in her approach to coaching.
