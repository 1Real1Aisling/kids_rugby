Youth Rugby Privacy Policy

At Youth Rugby, we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our website. Please read this policy carefully before using our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information from our users:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, and other information you provide when you register or make a purchase on our website.
  • Usage Information: This includes data about how you use our website, such as your browsing history, search history, and IP address.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to track your preferences and enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small data files that are stored on your device when you access our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To process your orders and purchases on our website.
  • To communicate with you about our products, services, and promotions.
  • To improve our website and enhance your user experience.
  • To comply with legal requirements and protect our rights.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your personal information with third parties for the following reasons:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with our service providers who assist us in processing your orders and providing our services.
  • Legal Requirements: We may share your information if required by law, such as in response to a subpoena or court order.
  • Business Transactions: We may share your information in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You may request access to the personal information we have collected about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information.
  • Object: You may object to the processing of your personal information for certain purposes.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time.
