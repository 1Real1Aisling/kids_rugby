Club Ethos

At our youth rugby club, we believe that rugby is more than just a sport – it’s a way of life. We are committed to building a positive and inclusive community where players of all ages and abilities can develop their skills, make lifelong friendships, and have fun on and off the field.

Our Four Goals

  1. Develop Players: We strive to develop players at all levels by providing a safe, inclusive, and enjoyable environment for them to learn and develop their rugby skills.
  2. Build Community: We aim to build a strong sense of community within the club, by encouraging active participation from players, coaches, volunteers, and parents alike.
  3. Promote Sportsmanship: We believe that rugby should be played with integrity and respect, and we aim to promote these values by encouraging fair play, respect for opponents, and graciousness in both victory and defeat.
  4. Inspire Success: We are committed to helping our players achieve success both on and off the field, by instilling a strong work ethic, promoting personal responsibility, and providing opportunities for personal growth and development.

Our Core Values

Respect

Sportsmanship

We firmly believe that rugby should be played with integrity, honor, and respect, and we work tirelessly to instill these values in all of our players. We encourage fair play, respect for opponents, and graciousness in both victory and defeat. Above all, we are here to inspire success. We believe that every player has the potential to achieve greatness, and we are committed to helping them reach their full potential, both on and off the pitch.

Join us, and become part of a community that is dedicated to rugby, growth, and success.
