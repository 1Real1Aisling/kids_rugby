Our programmes

Our youth rugby programs are fun, safe, and inclusive, designed to develop physical, social, and mental skills for all abilities. With experienced coaches, your child will grow their skills, confidence, teamwork, and leadership. Our programs offer new friendships, cultural experiences, and discipline. Explore our options for different age groups and abilities and find the perfect fit. Don't wait, contact us today to experience the joy of youth rugby. Our dedicated coaches and staff provide a positive, inclusive experience for all.

Ages 4-7

Mini Rugby

Mini Rugby is a non-contact version of rugby that is perfect for younger children in Ireland. It focuses on the basic skills of catching, passing, and running with the ball, and is played with a flag belt instead of tackling.

Ages 8-12

Youth Rugby

Youth Rugby is an introductory program for children who are ready to take their rugby skills to the next level. It focuses on the basic techniques of tackling, rucking, and scrumming in a safe and controlled environment.

Ages 13-18

Schools Rugby

Schools Rugby is designed for students who are serious about rugby and want to take their skills to a competitive level. It includes advanced training, tactical analysis, and participation in local and national school competitions.

Ages 13-18

Girls Rugby

Girls Rugby is a program designed specifically for girls who are interested in rugby. It focuses on the unique needs and abilities of female players, and includes training, games, and competitions against other girls teams.

Ages 16-18

Provincial Academy

The Provincial Academy is a program for elite players who are identified as having the potential to play at a high level. It includes advanced training, tactical analysis, and participation in provincial and national competitions.

Ages 16-18

Girls Rugby Development Squad

This program is designed for girls who want to take their rugby skills to the next level. The program includes participation in local and national girls rugby competitions to help players gain valuable experience and exposure to high-level competition.
